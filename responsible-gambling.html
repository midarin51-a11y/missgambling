Responsible Gambling

Gambling should be entertainment — not a way to make money.

Quick safety tips

  • Set a budget and time limit before you start.
  • Never chase losses.
  • Take breaks and keep balance with other activities.
  • If gambling stops being fun, stop and seek support.
